History

Rathinavel Subramaniam College of Arts and Science is started as first self-financing college under Bharathiar University in 1986. Within a brief span of two decades the college has developed as one of the largest self-financing colleges in Coimbatore region in terms of student strength, number of academic programmes and faculty strength. The college is managed by RVS Trust.

Courses

1)Fifteen undergraduate courses including arts and science courses like B.Com., Life Sciences, BCA, Psychology, English, and Management, 2)BSc courses in Mathematics, Biochemistry, Biotechnology, Microbiology, Nutrition and Dietetics, Data Science, Electronics, IoT and Computer Science, 3) Fourteen postgraduate programmes, 4)M.Phil and Ph.D programmes.

Timeline

1)1986: College established. 2)2001: The college got NAAC accreditation at four star level. 3)2004: Autonomous status confirmed in September. 4)2007: Attained B++ status on reaccreditation. 5)2014: Reaccrediated with "A" grade by NAAC. 6)2023: Reaccrediated with "A+" grade by NAAC.
